WISH, PLEASE

The application provides an opportunity to play the original jenga with desires. Rules of life:

1) Specify the number of players

2) Specify the names of the players

3) Add your wishes

When pulling out one bar, the application will give out a wish that needs to be fulfilled.

If the player has completed the task, you can click anywhere and the next wish for the next player will appear. If the tower has collapsed, you need to press the end to count points and identify the winner.

Good luck and have fun!

Number of players: 2
